The Editorial Team

Dr. Ramdas Pisharody

Principal

Medical College, Thiruvananthapuram

"It gives me immense pleasure to know that the Department of General Surgery, Medical College, Trivandrum is bringing out its own magazine named ‘Chirurgiae’. It is a novel and welcome venture and I’m sure that it will enrich the academic pursuits of the surgical residents. Such humble collection of case series have earlier paved way to world renowned biomedical literature such as Mayo Clinic Proceedings. I wish this venture all success and congratulate the Head of Surgery and the editorial team behind it."

"I'm extremely delighted to present 'Chirurgiae', the Department of Surgery Magazine - 2014. It will be a platform for the residents to take the first step towards academic publishing. I wish all success for the magazine and congratulate the editorial team for their painstaking effort in bringing out the same."

Dr. Sreekumar A, Emeritus Editor

Professor and Head, Department of Surgery
Medical College, Thiruvananthapuram
