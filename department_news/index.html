Department News

Peripheral Meet - 2014

Association of Surgeons of India, Kerala Chapter Was held on 22nd and 23rd February 2014 at Kovalam. The ASI President elect, Dr.Santosh John Abraham was honoured at the function.

Dr. Rakhul.L.R & Dr. Shafeek.S came 1st in the ASI National level Quiz.

The Department celebrated the annual General surgical week ‘Anastomosis’ and honoured our beloved Head of the Department, Dr. A.Sreekumar sir on the 30th year of his teaching career. Various games, sports and cultural events were conducted with exuberant participation from the staff and residents alike.
